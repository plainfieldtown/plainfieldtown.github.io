ABOUT OUR COMMUNITY

Plainfield, established during the great mining boom, stands as a testament to Midwestern resilience. Though our mines have closed, our spirit remains strong as we face the challenges of the coming millennium together.

Population: 2,014 (1998 Est.)
Founded: 1878 (Mining Charter)
Mayor: Richard Wilson (1996-Present)
